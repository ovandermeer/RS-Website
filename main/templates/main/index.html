Basedism

Basedism is a new-age religion with a focus on meditation and community. There are no requirements to join and we strongly believe in radical acceptance, the practice of not juding others for their immutable traits. Regardless of gender, sexuality, race, ethnicity or history, we accept and celebrate all.
